Cricket
Health benefits of cricket Although there is some standing around, to play cricket you need to be fit and strong, and have good hand-eye coordination and ball-handling skills. Cricket involves sprinting between wickets and running to stop balls, as well as bowling and throwing. (a).Health benefits include:
I. Endurance and stamina
II. Balance and coordination
III. Physical fitness
IV. Improving hand-eye coordination.
(b) Other benefits of cricket
I. Team skills
II Social skills such as cooperation, communication and learning how to cope with winning and losing
